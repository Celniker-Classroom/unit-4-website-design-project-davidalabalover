Premier League Legends

The Premier League's history is defined by legendary figures like Thierry Henry, widely considered the greatest for his 175 goals at Arsenal, and Alan Shearer, who holds the all-time scoring record with 260 goals. Midfield has been dominated by icons like Ryan Giggs, alongside powerhouse scorers like Frank Lampard and creative geniuses like Paul Scholes and Steven Gerrard. In more recent years, Cristiano Ronaldo reached Ballon d’Or heights at Manchester United, while modern stars like Kevin De Bruyne and Mohamed Salah have set new benchmarks for assists and winger goalscoring. Today, Erling Haaland continues to shatter records with his clinical finishing, ensuring the league remains home to the world's most elite talent. As for the defence, Legends such as Rio Ferdinand, John Terry, and Virgil Van Dijk lead the league. As of now, Arsenal is widely known for having the best defence in the league including world stars such as Gabriel and William Saliba.
