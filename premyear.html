This year, commanding teams such as Arsenal have a 9 point lead at the top with 70, just behind Manchester City. At the opposite side of the table, Wolverhampton Wanderers are last, with only 17 points, 12 behind non relegation. Sunderland, who joined the premier league for the first time in 10 years, were expected to go right back down. However, they currently sit in 13th place, with wins against Crystal palace, Chelsea, and Newcastle, and draws against Arsenal and Liverpool. Tottenham, who are a one of the most popular clubs in all of england, are having a horrible season, sitting only 1 point away from being relegated. The top scorer is Erling Haaland, and the top assister in Bruno Fernandes. Some promising youngsters have been Rio Ngumoha from Liverpool, Max Dowmann from Arsenal, who has become the youngest Premier League goal scorer, and Estevao from Chelsea.
